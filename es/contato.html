Información Importante

GitTree is maintained by a small independent team and operated as a free service. To set clear expectations, here is what falls within and outside our support scope:

Outside our scope:

  • Individual or dedicated technical support agreements
  • Real-time support (live chat, phone, or video calls)
  • Personalized consulting or code review services
  • Analysis of private or authentication-gated repositories
  • Guaranteed SLA or uptime commitments

Within our scope:

  • Clarifications about how the tool works and how to interpret results
  • Evaluation and prioritization of improvement suggestions
  • Acknowledgement and resolution of reported bugs
  • Clarifications about data handling, privacy policy, and terms of use
  • Contributions, pull requests, and open-source collaboration
